What we have done

Environmental Monitoring System

Sri Lanka is a heavily affected area for adverse weather events. So, we at Faculty of IoT labs, designed a cost effective solution to overcome the huge costs related to the implementation of weather stations islandwide while keeping the accuracy at maximum levels

Air Quality Measurement System

Outdoor air pollution has become a pressing matter in urban areas. So, we at Faculty of IT IoT Labs, designed a air quality monitoring system to inform the public about air quality status of Colombo Megapolis Area.

University Access Control System

A fully automated access control system for University of Moratuwa students using RFID powered student ID cards to ensure the maximum security of University of Moratuwa premises and students.
